Hope is a light in the darkest night. A quiet spark, gentle and bright. It whispers softly, “Don’t give in.”
         
           And wakes the strength that lies within. When storms arrive and skies turn gray. Hope finds a quiet,
            
                    steady way. It walks beside us through the rain. And slowly softens every pain.
         
               Optimism rises like the sun. Reminding us we’ve just begun. It paints tomorrow clear and new.
          
                    With endless skies of hopeful blue. So hold on tight when days feel long.
        
        Let hope become your quiet song. For every night will fade away. And lead the heart to brighter day. ✨
